About this tool

This tool enables you to add or remove gear to previous activities in one go. You can configure a date range or just add to all activities. We can process up to 500 activities every 15 minutes due to limits imposed on us by Strava. If you have more activities than this limit you can do it in several parts. We are not affiliated or endorsed by Strava.

Get started

Click the button below so we can access and update your activities. This will take you to Strava's website to login and grant us permission. If you only want public activities updated you can untick View data about your private activities. The other two boxes must be ticked for the tool to work. You will then be able to select and add your gear.
